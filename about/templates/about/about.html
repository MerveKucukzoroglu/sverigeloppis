FAQ

HOW TO ADD A LOPPIS?

Steps to securely add your loppis announcement:

  1. Sigin to your account, register if you don't have one yet.
  2. Go to 'Add Loppis' page
  3. Fill in the form the required information to add your loppis.
  4. Each Loppis announcement costs 5 Swedish Krona (SEK).
  5. Fill in your information to pay.
  6. Once the payment is completed, you will receive an email about confirmation and your loppis will be added to the All Loppis page.
  7. You can find the list of your published loppis announcements in your Profile page and manage them as you wish.
